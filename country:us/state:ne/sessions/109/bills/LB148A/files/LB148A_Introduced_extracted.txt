LEGISLATURE OF NEBRASKA
ONE HUNDRED NINTH LEGISLATURE
FIRST SESSION
Introduced by Hansen, 16.
Read first time March 12, 2025
1 A BILL FOR AN ACT relating to appropriations; to appropriate funds to aid
2 in carrying out the provisions of Legislative Bill 148, One Hundred
3 Ninth Legislature, First Session, 2025.
4 Be it enacted by the people of the State of Nebraska,
-1-
LB148A LB148A
2025 2025
1 Section 1. There is hereby appropriated (1) $29,629 from the General
2 Fund and $28,236 from the Nebraska State Patrol Cash Fund for FY2025-26
3 and (2) $30,921 from the General Fund and $28,236 from the Nebraska State
4 Patrol Cash Fund for FY2026-27 to the Nebraska State Patrol, for Program
5 100, to aid in carrying out the provisions of Legislative Bill 148, One
6 Hundred Ninth Legislature, First Session, 2025.
7 Total expenditures for permanent and temporary salaries and per
8 diems from funds appropriated in this section shall not exceed $33,856
9 for FY2025-26 or $34,872 for FY2026-27.
