1 A BILL FOR AN ACT relating to the Game and Parks Commission; to amend
2 section 37-101, Reissue Revised Statutes of Nebraska; to change
3 provisions relating to term limits; to eliminate obsolete
4 provisions; and to repeal the original section.
5 Be it enacted by the people of the State of Nebraska,
-1-
LB377 LB377
2025 2025
1 Section 1. Section 37-101, Reissue Revised Statutes of Nebraska, is
2 amended to read:
3 37-101 (1)(a) The Game and Parks Commission shall consist of nine
4 members, one from each of the eight districts provided for by section
5 37-102 and one at-large member, and shall be appointed by the Governor
6 with the consent of a majority of all members of the Legislature.
7 (b) Members of the commission shall be legal residents and citizens
8 of Nebraska and shall be well informed and interested in matters under
9 the jurisdiction of the commission.
10 (c) At least three members of the commission shall be actually
11 engaged in agricultural pursuits.
12 (d) Not more than five of the members of the commission shall be
13 affiliated with the same political party. The political party affiliation
14 of each prospective member shall be determined as of the statewide
15 general election prior to his or her appointment.
16 (e) Members of the commission representing districts provided for by
17 section 37-102 shall be bona fide residents of the district from which
18 they are appointed.
19 (f) When a member ceases to be a member of the political party
20 determined under subdivision (d) of this subsection, ceases to be a bona
21 fide resident of the district, or ceases to be actually engaged in
22 agricultural pursuits if required to meet the qualifications for his or
23 her appointment, the office shall be immediately vacated.
24 (2)(a) When the term of a member of the commission representing a
25 district provided for by section 37-102 expires, a successor shall be
26 appointed as provided in subsection (1) of this section from the same
27 district as the member whose term has expired. The terms of the members
28 serving district numbers 4, 6, and 8 on January 1, 2009, shall be
29 extended to January 15 of the year following the expiration of their
30 current terms. Members appointed for terms expiring prior to January 1,
31 2012, shall be appointed for five-year terms. Members appointed for terms
-2-
LB377 LB377
2025 2025
1 expiring on or after January 1, 2012, shall be appointed for four-year
2 terms. The terms of all members shall begin on January 15 , and the term
3 of the at-large member shall begin January 15, 2009.
4 (b) Each member shall serve until the appointment and qualification
5 of his or her successor. In case of a vacancy occurring prior to the
6 expiration of the term of a member, the appointment shall be made only
7 for the remainder of the term. An appointment made for the remainder of
8 the term shall not be considered a full term.
9 (c) No person who has served three two full terms shall be eligible
10 for reappointment as a member of the commission.
11 (3) If the Legislature is not in session when members of the
12 commission are appointed by the Governor, they shall take office and act
13 as recess appointees until the Legislature next thereafter convenes.
14 (4) Members may be removed by the Governor for inefficiency, neglect
15 of duty, or misconduct in office, but only after delivering to the member
16 a copy of the charges and affording an opportunity of being publicly
17 heard in person or by counsel in his or her own defense, upon not less
18 than ten days' notice. Such hearing shall be held before the Governor.
19 If such member is removed, the Governor shall file in the office of
20 the Secretary of State a complete statement of all charges made against
21 such member and his or her findings thereon, together with a complete
22 record of the proceedings.
23 Sec. 2. Original section 37-101, Reissue Revised Statutes of
24 Nebraska, is repealed.
