Section 1:
LB170A LB170A
2025 2025
LEGISLATURE OF NEBRASKA
ONE HUNDRED NINTH LEGISLATURE
FIRST SESSION
Introduced by Brandt, 32.
Read first time May 19, 2025
1 A BILL FOR AN ACT relating to appropriations; to appropriate funds to aid
2 in carrying out the provisions of Legislative Bill 170, One Hundred
3 Ninth Legislature, First Session, 2025; and to declare an emergency.
4 Be it enacted by the people of the State of Nebraska,
-1-
LB170A LB170A
2025 2025
1 Section 1. The State Treasurer shall transfer $100,000,000 from the
2 General Fund to the School District Property Tax Relief Credit Fund on or
3 after July 1, 2025, but before June 30, 2026, on such dates and in such
4 amounts as directed by the budget administrator of the budget division of
5 the Department of Administrative Services.
6 Sec. 2. The State Treasurer shall transfer $100,000,000 from the
7 General Fund to the School District Property Tax Relief Credit Fund on or
8 after July 1, 2026, but before June 30, 2027, on such dates and in such
9 amounts as directed by the budget administrator of the budget division of
10 the Department of Administrative Services.
11 Sec. 3. There is hereby appropriated (1) $100,000,000 from the
12 School District Property Tax Relief Credit Fund for FY2025-26 and (2)
13 $100,000,000 from the School District Property Tax Relief Credit Fund for
14 FY2026-27 to the Department of Revenue, for Program 121, to aid in
15 carrying out the provisions of Legislative Bill 170, One Hundred Ninth
16 Legislature, First Session, 2025.
17 There is included in the appropriation to this program for FY2025-26
18 $100,000,000 Cash Funds for state aid, which shall only be used for such
19 purpose. There is included in the appropriation to this program for
20 FY2026-27 $100,000,000 Cash Funds for state aid, which shall only be used
21 for such purpose.
22 No expenditures for permanent and temporary salaries and per diems
23 for state employees shall be made from funds appropriated in this
24 section.
25 Sec. 4. Since an emergency exists, this act takes effect when
26 passed and approved according to law.
