ONE HUNDRED NINTH LEGISLATURE
FIRST SESSION
LEGISLATIVE RESOLUTION 268
WHEREAS, on January 16, 2025, the home of Kevin Faimon went ablaze with
him, his son, Hunter Faimon, and his dog, MJ, inside; and
WHEREAS, Hastings firefighters Chance Bronson, Jonas Marshall, Colby
Jensen, and Thomas Treffler bravely entered the residence of Kevin Faimon; and
WHEREAS, the lives of Kevin, Hunter, and MJ Faimon were saved due to the
heroic and selfless actions of the members of Hastings Fire and Rescue.
NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE MEMBERS OF THE ONE HUNDRED NINTH
LEGISLATURE OF NEBRASKA, FIRST SESSION:

Section 2:
1. That the Legislature extends its gratitude towards the brave actions
taken by Chance Bronson, Jonas Marshall, Colby Jensen, and Thomas Treffler.

Section 3:
2. That copies of this resolution be sent to Chance Bronson, Jonas
Marshall, Colby Jensen, and Thomas Treffler.

LEGISLATIVE RESOLUTION 268
Introduced by Murman, 38; Lonowski, 33; Storm, 23; Wordekemper, 15.

Jensen, and Thomas Treffler bravely entered the residence of Kevin Faimon; and

heroic and selfless actions of the members of Hastings Fire and Rescue.

LEGISLATURE OF NEBRASKA, FIRST SESSION:
1. That the Legislature extends its gratitude towards the brave actions
taken by Chance Bronson, Jonas Marshall, Colby Jensen, and Thomas Treffler.
2. That copies of this resolution be sent to Chance Bronson, Jonas
Marshall, Colby Jensen, and Thomas Treffler.
