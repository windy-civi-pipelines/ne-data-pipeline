ONE HUNDRED NINTH LEGISLATURE
FIRST SESSION
LEGISLATIVE RESOLUTION 271
Meyer, 17; Storer, 43.
WHEREAS, Nebraska's FFA chapters play a vital role in the creation of
Nebraska's future agricultural economy; and
WHEREAS, FFA advisors are essential to providing guidance, instruction,
and mentorship to students participating in FFA; and
WHEREAS, Ben Robison of Alma, Nebraska, was recognized as a 2025 FFA
Advisor of the Year by the Nebraska Farm Bureau Foundation for his leadership.
NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE MEMBERS OF THE ONE HUNDRED NINTH
LEGISLATURE OF NEBRASKA, FIRST SESSION:

Section 2:
1. That the Legislature congratulates Ben Robison for earning the 2025 FFA
Advisor of the Year award.

Section 3:
2. That a copy of this resolution be sent to Ben Robison.
