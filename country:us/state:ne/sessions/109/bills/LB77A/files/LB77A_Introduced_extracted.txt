LEGISLATURE OF NEBRASKA
ONE HUNDRED NINTH LEGISLATURE
FIRST SESSION
Introduced by Bostar, 29.
Read first time May 14, 2025
1 A BILL FOR AN ACT relating to appropriations; to appropriate funds to aid
2 in carrying out the provisions of Legislative Bill 77, One Hundred
3 Ninth Legislature, First Session, 2025.
4 Be it enacted by the people of the State of Nebraska,
-1-
LB77A LB77A
2025 2025
1 Section 1. There is hereby appropriated (1) $500,00 from the
2 Department of Insurance Cash Fund for FY2025-26 and (2) $500,000 from the
3 Department of Insurance Cash Fund for FY2026-27 to the Department of
4 Insurance, for Program 69, to aid in carrying out the provisions of
5 Legislative Bill 77, One Hundred Ninth Legislature, First Session, 2025.
6 No expenditures for permanent and temporary salaries and per diems
7 for state employees shall be made from funds appropriated in this
8 section.
