LB382A LB382A
2025 2025
LEGISLATURE OF NEBRASKA
ONE HUNDRED NINTH LEGISLATURE
FIRST SESSION
Introduced by Meyer, 17.
Read first time April 22, 2025
1 A BILL FOR AN ACT relating to appropriations; to appropriate funds to aid
2 in carrying out the provisions of Legislative Bill 382, One Hundred
3 Ninth Legislature, First Session, 2025.
4 Be it enacted by the people of the State of Nebraska,
-1-
LB382A LB382A
2025 2025
1 Section 1. There is hereby appropriated (1) $1,000,000 from the
2 Medicaid Managed Care Excess Profit Fund for FY2025-26 and (2) $1,000,000
3 from the Medicaid Managed Care Excess Profit Fund for FY2026-27 to the
4 Department of Health and Human Services, for Program 33, to aid in
5 carrying out the provisions of Legislative Bill 382, One Hundred Ninth
6 Legislature, First Session, 2025.
7 Total expenditures for permanent and temporary salaries and per
8 diems from funds appropriated in this section shall not exceed $60,000
9 for FY2025-26 or $60,000 for FY2026-27.
10 Sec. 2. There is hereby appropriated (1) $2,000,000 from the
11 Medicaid Managed Care Excess Profit Fund for FY2025-26 and (2) $2,000,000
12 from the Medicaid Managed Care Excess Profit Fund for FY2026-27 to the
13 Department of Health and Human Services, for Program 571, to aid in
14 carrying out the provisions of Legislative Bill 382, One Hundred Ninth
15 Legislature, First Session, 2025.
16 There is included in the appropriation to this program for FY2025-26
17 $2,000,000 cash funds for state aid, which shall only be used for such
18 purpose. There is included in the appropriation to this program for
19 FY2026-27 $2,000,000 cash funds for state aid, which shall only be used
20 for such purpose.
21 No expenditures for permanent and temporary salaries and per diems
22 for state employees shall be made from funds appropriated in this
23 section.
