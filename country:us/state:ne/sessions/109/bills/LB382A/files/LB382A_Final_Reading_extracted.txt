LEGISLATURE OF NEBRASKA
ONE HUNDRED NINTH LEGISLATURE
FIRST SESSION
FINAL READING
Introduced by Meyer, 17.
Read first time April 22, 2025
1 A BILL FOR AN ACT relating to appropriations; to appropriate funds to aid
2 in carrying out the provisions of Legislative Bill 382, One Hundred
3 Ninth Legislature, First Session, 2025.
4 Be it enacted by the people of the State of Nebraska,
-1-
LB382A LB382A
2025 2025
1 Section 1. There is hereby appropriated (1) $2,000,000 from the
2 Medicaid Managed Care Excess Profit Fund for FY2025-26 and (2) $2,000,000
3 from the Medicaid Managed Care Excess Profit Fund for FY2026-27 to the
4 Department of Health and Human Services, for Program 571, to aid in
5 carrying out the provisions of Legislative Bill 382, One Hundred Ninth
6 Legislature, First Session, 2025.
7 There is included in the appropriation to this program for FY2025-26
8 $2,000,000 Cash Funds for state aid, which shall only be used for such
9 purpose. There is included in the appropriation to this program for
10 FY2026-27 $2,000,000 Cash Funds for state aid, which shall only be used
11 for such purpose.
12 No expenditures for permanent and temporary salaries and per diems
13 for state employees shall be made from funds appropriated in this
14 section.
