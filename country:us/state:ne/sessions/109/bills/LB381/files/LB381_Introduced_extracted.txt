7 cost-avoidance through identification of third-party liability, (b) cost

9 casualty recovery of payments by identifying and recovering costs for

12 services or other individuals furnishing items and services for which
13 payment has been made to determine whether providers have been underpaid

22 payment. After one year from the date of payment, a payment shall not be

24 (c) (b) Send a determination letter concluding an audit within one
25 hundred eighty days after receipt of all requested material from a
26 provider;
