LEGISLATURE OF NEBRASKA
ONE HUNDRED NINTH LEGISLATURE
FIRST SESSION
Introduced by Sanders, 45.

Committee: Government, Military and Veterans Affairs
1 A BILL FOR AN ACT relating to county government; to amend section 23-367,
2 Reissue Revised Statutes of Nebraska; to change a provision relating
3 to special sidewalk assessments; and to repeal the original section.
4 Be it enacted by the people of the State of Nebraska,
-1-
LB243 LB243
2025 2025
1 Section 1. Section 23-367, Reissue Revised Statutes of Nebraska, is
2 amended to read:
3 23-367 Special sidewalk assessments may be collected in the manner
4 usual for the collection or foreclosure of county or state taxes against
5 real estate.
6 Sec. 2. Original section 23-367, Reissue Revised Statutes of
7 Nebraska, is repealed.
