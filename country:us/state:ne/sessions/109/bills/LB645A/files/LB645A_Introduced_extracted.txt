LEGISLATURE OF NEBRASKA
ONE HUNDRED NINTH LEGISLATURE
FIRST SESSION
Introduced by Ballard, 21.
Read first time April 23, 2025
1 A BILL FOR AN ACT relating to appropriations; to appropriate funds to aid
2 in carrying out the provisions of Legislative Bill 645, One Hundred
3 Ninth Legislature, First Session, 2025; and to declare an emergency.
4 Be it enacted by the people of the State of Nebraska,
-1-
LB645A LB645A
2025 2025
1 Section 1. There is hereby appropriated (1) $18,037 from the School
2 Expense Fund for FY2025-26 and (2) $-0- from the School Expense Fund for
3 FY2026-27 to the Public Employees Retirement Board, for Program 41, to
4 aid in carrying out the provisions of Legislative Bill 645, One Hundred
5 Ninth Legislature, First Session, 2025.
6 No expenditures for permanent and temporary salaries and per diems
7 for state employees shall be made from funds appropriated in this
8 section.
9 Sec. 2. Since an emergency exists, this act takes effect when
10 passed and approved according to law.
