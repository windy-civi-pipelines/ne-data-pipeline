LB230A LB230A
2025 2025
LEGISLATURE OF NEBRASKA
ONE HUNDRED NINTH LEGISLATURE
FIRST SESSION
Introduced by Hallstrom, 1.
Read first time April 01, 2025
1 A BILL FOR AN ACT relating to appropriations; to appropriate funds to aid
2 in carrying out the provisions of Legislative Bill 230, One Hundred
3 Ninth Legislature, First Session, 2025; and to declare an emergency.
4 Be it enacted by the people of the State of Nebraska,
-1-
LB230A LB230A
2025 2025
1 Section 1. There is hereby appropriated (1) $251,010 from the
2 Department of Revenue Enforcement Fund for FY2025-26 and (2) $121,600
3 from the Department of Revenue Enforcement Fund for FY2026-27 to the
4 Department of Revenue, for Program 102, to aid in carrying out the
5 provisions of Legislative Bill 230, One Hundred Ninth Legislature, First
6 Session, 2025.
7 Total expenditures for permanent and temporary salaries and per
8 diems from funds appropriated in this section shall not exceed $88,600
9 for FY2025-26 or $91,400 for FY2026-27.
10 Sec. 2. Since an emergency exists, this act takes effect when
11 passed and approved according to law.
-2-
