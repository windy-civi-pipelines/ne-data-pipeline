LEGISLATURE OF NEBRASKA
ONE HUNDRED NINTH LEGISLATURE
FIRST SESSION
FINAL READING
Introduced by Riepe, 12.
Read first time January 13, 2025
Committee: Health and Human Services
1 A BILL FOR AN ACT relating to the practice of barbering; to amend section
2 71-216.01, Reissue Revised Statutes of Nebraska; to change
3 requirements for applicants taking the barber examination; and to
4 repeal the original section.
5 Be it enacted by the people of the State of Nebraska,
-1-
LB160 LB160
2025 2025
1 Section 1. Section 71-216.01, Reissue Revised Statutes of Nebraska,
2 is amended to read:
3 71-216.01 (1) An applicant who fails to pass the barber examination
4 may take the examination up to two additional times when the examination
5 is offered by the board without being required to take any further course
6 of study. If an applicant fails the examination a third time, the
7 applicant shall be required to complete a further course of study in
8 barbering, consisting of five hundred hours with not more than ten hours
9 completed in any one working day in a school of barbering prior to
10 further examination.
11 (2) An applicant may be given an opportunity to demonstrate
12 proficiency to waive the remainder of the course of study after
13 completion of two hundred fifty hours with the approval of the board. If
14 such applicant fails the examination after being given such opportunity,
15 the applicant shall complete the remaining two hundred fifty hours before
16 such applicant shall be permitted to take the examination a fifth time.
17 A graduate from a school of barbering who fails to pass a
18 satisfactory examination may take the examination next time that the
19 examination is given by the Board of Barber Examiners without being
20 required to take any further course of study. Should the applicant fail
21 the examination a second time, the applicant shall be required to
22 complete a further course of study of not less than five hundred hours to
23 be completed within three months of not more than ten hours in any one
24 working day in a school of barbering approved by the Board of Barber
25 Examiners before the applicant may be permitted to take the examination a
26 third time.
27 Sec. 2. Original section 71-216.01, Reissue Revised Statutes of
28 Nebraska, is repealed.
