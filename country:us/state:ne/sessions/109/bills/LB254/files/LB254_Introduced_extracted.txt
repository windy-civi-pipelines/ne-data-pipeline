1 A BILL FOR AN ACT relating to appropriations; to state legislative intent
2 to transfer funds for workforce housing; to declare an emergency.

-1-
LB254 LB254
2025 2025
1 Section 1. It is the intent of the Legislature to transfer twenty-
2 five million dollars from the General Fund for fiscal year 2025-26 to the
3 Rural Workforce Housing Investment Fund and twenty-five million dollars
4 from the General Fund for fiscal year 2026-27 to the Rural Workforce
5 Housing Investment Fund to provide workforce housing grants under the
6 Rural Workforce Housing Investment Act.
7 Sec. 2. Since an emergency exists, this act takes effect when
8 passed and approved according to law.
