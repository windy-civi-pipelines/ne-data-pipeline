1 A BILL FOR AN ACT relating to postsecondary education; to amend sections
2 85-106, 85-304, 85-1511, and 85-1530, Reissue Revised Statutes of
3 Nebraska; to prohibit the practice of academic tenure at
4 postsecondary educational institutions governed by the Board of
5 Regents of the University of Nebraska, the Board of Trustees of the
6 Nebraska State Colleges, or a community college board of governors

8 prescribed; to provide powers and duties to the Board of Regents of
9 the University of Nebraska, the Board of Trustees of the Nebraska
10 State Colleges, and any community college board of governors; to
11 harmonize provisions; and to repeal the original sections.

23 consistent with sections 82-317 to 82-329 and 85-106.01 to 85-106.03.
