LB284 LB284
2025 2025
LEGISLATURE OF NEBRASKA
ONE HUNDRED NINTH LEGISLATURE
FIRST SESSION
Introduced by Guereca, 7.
Read first time January 15, 2025
Committee: Appropriations
1 A BILL FOR AN ACT relating to appropriations; to appropriate funds to the
2 Department of Health and Human Services; and to declare an
3 emergency.
4 Be it enacted by the people of the State of Nebraska,
-1-
LB284 LB284
2025 2025
1 Section 1. There is hereby appropriated (1) $500,000 from the
2 Health and Human Services Cash Fund for FY2025-26 and (2) $500,000 from
3 the Health and Human Services Cash Fund for FY2026-27 to the Department
4 of Health and Human Services, for Program 514, for state aid to a
5 nonprofit organization holding a certificate of exemption under section
6 501(c)(3) of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 providing health care
7 screening, sports competitions, educational opportunities, and leadership
8 training for persons with developmental or intellectual disabilities.
9 There is included in the appropriation to this program for FY2025-26
10 $500,000 Cash Funds for state aid, which shall only be used for such
11 purpose. There is included in the appropriation to this program for
12 FY2026-27 $500,000 Cash Funds for state aid, which shall only be used for
13 such purpose.
14 No expenditures for permanent and temporary salaries and per diems
15 for state employees shall be made from funds appropriated in this
16 section.
17 Sec. 2. Since an emergency exists, this act takes effect when
18 passed and approved according to law.
