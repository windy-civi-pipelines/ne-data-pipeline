AMENDMENTS TO LB303
Introduced by Hughes, 24.
1 1. On page 1, strike lines 10 through 14 and insert the following
2 new subdivision:
3 "(d) Three members of the Legislature, who shall be nonvoting
4 members of the commission, including the Chairperson of the Education
5 Committee of the Legislature or the chairperson's designee, the
6 Chairperson of the Revenue Committee of the Legislature or the
7 chairperson's designee, and one member appointed pursuant to subsection
8 (2) of this section. No more than two members shall be affiliated with
9 the same political party;".
10 2. On page 2, strike lines 10 through 15 and insert the following
11 new subdivision:
12 "(2)(a) The member to be appointed as described in subdivision (1)
13 (d) of this section shall be appointed by the Executive Board of the
14 Legislative Council in January of each odd-numbered year for a two-year
15 term, except that the initial member shall be appointed within thirty
16 days after the effective date of this act and shall serve until a
17 successor is appointed in January 2027.".
