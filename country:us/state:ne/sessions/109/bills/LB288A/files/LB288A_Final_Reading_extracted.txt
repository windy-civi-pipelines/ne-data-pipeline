1 A BILL FOR AN ACT relating to appropriations; to appropriate funds to aid
2 in carrying out the provisions of Legislative Bill 288, One Hundred
3 Ninth Legislature, First Session, 2025.
4 Be it enacted by the people of the State of Nebraska,
-1-
LB288A LB288A
2025 2025
1 Section 1. There is hereby appropriated (1) $97,030 from the Middle
2 Income Workforce Housing Investment Fund and $103,200 from the Affordable
3 Housing Trust Fund for FY2025-26 and (2) $118,110 from the Middle Income
4 Workforce Housing Investment Fund and $126,410 from the Affordable
5 Housing Trust Fund for FY2026-27 to the Department of Economic
6 Development, for Program 601, to aid in carrying out the provisions of
7 Legislative Bill 288, One Hundred Ninth Legislature, First Session, 2025.
8 Total expenditures for permanent and temporary salaries and per
9 diems from funds appropriated in this section shall not exceed $103,520
10 for FY2025-26 or $138,030 for FY2026-27.
