LB505 LB505
2025 2025
LEGISLATURE OF NEBRASKA
ONE HUNDRED NINTH LEGISLATURE
FIRST SESSION
Introduced by Prokop, 27; DeBoer, 10.
Read first time January 21, 2025
Committee: Appropriations
1 A BILL FOR AN ACT relating to appropriations; to state intent regarding
2 appropriations for food assistance; and to declare an emergency.
3 Be it enacted by the people of the State of Nebraska,
-1-
LB505 LB505
2025 2025
1 Section 1. It is the intent of the Legislature to appropriate
2 $10,000,000 for FY2025-26 and $10,000,000 for FY2026-27 from Federal
3 Funds to the Department of Health and Human Services, Program No. 347,
4 Public Assistance. There is included in the appropriation to the program
5 funds from the federal Temporary Assistance for Needy Families program
6 for grants to nonprofit organizations that (1) provide food assistance,
7 (2) distribute food in ten or more counties in Nebraska, and (3) qualify
8 for the Emergency Food Assistance Program administered by the United
9 States Department of Agriculture.
10 Sec. 2. Since an emergency exists, this act takes effect when
11 passed and approved according to law.
