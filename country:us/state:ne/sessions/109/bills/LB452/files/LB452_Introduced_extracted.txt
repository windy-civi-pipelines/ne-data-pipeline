LB452 LB452
2025 2025
LEGISLATURE OF NEBRASKA
ONE HUNDRED NINTH LEGISLATURE
FIRST SESSION
Introduced by Prokop, 27.
Read first time January 21, 2025
Committee: Appropriations
1 A BILL FOR AN ACT relating to appropriations; to state intent to
2 appropriate funds to the Department of Health and Human Services;
3 and to declare an emergency.
4 Be it enacted by the people of the State of Nebraska,
-1-
LB452 LB452
2025 2025
1 Section 1. It is the intent of the Legislature to appropriate
2 $500,000 for FY2025-26 and $500,000 for FY2026-27 from federal funds to
3 the Department of Health and Human Services for state aid.
4 There is included in the appropriation to this program for FY2025-26
5 $500,000 Federal Funds and for FY2026-27 $500,000 Federal Funds for court
6 appointed special advocate state aid, which shall only be used for such
7 purpose. There is included in the amount shown as aid for this program
8 $500,000 for FY2025-26 from federal Temporary Assistance for Needy
9 Families funds and $500,000 for FY2026-27 from federal Temporary
10 Assistance for Needy Families funds.
11 Sec. 2. Since an emergency exists, this act takes effect when
12 passed and approved according to law.
-2-
