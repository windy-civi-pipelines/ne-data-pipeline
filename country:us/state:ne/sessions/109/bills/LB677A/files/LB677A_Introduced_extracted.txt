LB677A LB677A
2025 2025
LEGISLATURE OF NEBRASKA
ONE HUNDRED NINTH LEGISLATURE
FIRST SESSION
Introduced by Hansen, 16.
Read first time May 15, 2025
1 A BILL FOR AN ACT relating to appropriations; to appropriate funds to aid
2 in carrying out the provisions of Legislative Bill 677, One Hundred
3 Ninth Legislature, First Session, 2025; and to declare an emergency.
4 Be it enacted by the people of the State of Nebraska,
-1-
LB677A LB677A
2025 2025
1 Section 1. There is hereby appropriated (1) $1 from the Medical
2 Cannabis Control Fund for FY2025-26 and (2) $1 from the Medical Cannabis
3 Control Fund for FY2026-27 to the Nebraska Liquor Control Commission, for
4 Program 96, to aid in carrying out the provisions of Legislative Bill
5 677, One Hundred Ninth Legislature, First Session, 2025.
6 Total expenditures for permanent and temporary salaries and per
7 diems from funds appropriated in this section shall not exceed $1 for
8 FY2025-26 or $1 for FY2026-27.
9 Sec. 2. Since an emergency exists, this act takes effect when
10 passed and approved according to law.
-2-
