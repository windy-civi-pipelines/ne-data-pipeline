ONE HUNDRED NINTH LEGISLATURE
FIRST SESSION
LEGISLATIVE RESOLUTION 272
Introduced by Prokop, 27.
Soccer State Championship was held on May 19, 2025, at Morrison Stadium in
Omaha, Nebraska; and
WHEREAS, the Lincoln Southwest High School Silver Hawks girls soccer team
competed in the Class A State Championship and secured a 2-1 victory over the
Omaha Marian Crusaders; and
WHEREAS, with this victory, the Silver Hawks won back-to-back Class A
state titles; and
WHEREAS, Lincoln Southwest concluded the season with an 18-1 record,
including twelve straight wins to close out the season; and
WHEREAS, such a team achievement is made possible through the support of
coaches, teachers, administrators, parents, and the community; and
WHEREAS, the Legislature recognizes the academic, athletic, and artistic
achievements of the youth of our state.
NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE MEMBERS OF THE ONE HUNDRED NINTH
LEGISLATURE OF NEBRASKA, FIRST SESSION:

Section 2:
1. That the Legislature congratulates the Lincoln Southwest High School
Silver Hawks girls soccer team for winning the 2025 Nebraska School Activities
Association Class A State Girls Soccer Championship.

Section 3:
2. That copies of this resolution be sent to the Lincoln Southwest High
School girls soccer team and head coach Thomas Nettleton.

Silver Hawks girls soccer team for winning the 2025 Nebraska School Activities
