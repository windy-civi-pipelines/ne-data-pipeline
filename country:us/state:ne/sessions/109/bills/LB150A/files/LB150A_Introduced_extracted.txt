LEGISLATURE OF NEBRASKA
ONE HUNDRED NINTH LEGISLATURE
FIRST SESSION
Introduced by Bosn, 25.
Read first time May 19, 2025
1 A BILL FOR AN ACT relating to appropriations; to appropriate funds to aid
2 in carrying out the provisions of Legislative Bill 150, One Hundred
3 Ninth Legislature, First Session, 2025.
4 Be it enacted by the people of the State of Nebraska,
-1-
LB150A LB150A
2025 2025
1 Section 1. There is hereby appropriated (1) $731,488 from federal
2 funds for FY2025-26 and (2) $762,807 from federal funds for FY2026-27 to
3 the Nebraska Commission on Law Enforcement and Criminal Justice, for
4 Program 155, to aid in carrying out the provisions of Legislative Bill
5 150, One Hundred Ninth Legislature, First Session, 2025.
6 Total expenditures for permanent and temporary salaries and per
7 diems from funds appropriated in this section shall not exceed $93,696
8 for FY2025-26 or $98,376 for FY2026-27.
