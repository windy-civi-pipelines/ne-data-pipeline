LB169A LB169A
2025 2025
LEGISLATURE OF NEBRASKA
ONE HUNDRED NINTH LEGISLATURE
FIRST SESSION
Introduced by Brandt, 32.
Read first time April 15, 2025
1 A BILL FOR AN ACT relating to appropriations; to appropriate funds to aid
2 in carrying out the provisions of Legislative Bill 169, One Hundred
3 Ninth Legislature, First Session, 2025; and to declare an emergency.
4 Be it enacted by the people of the State of Nebraska,
-1-
LB169A LB169A
2025 2025
1 Section 1. There is hereby appropriated (1) $278,900 from the
2 General Fund for FY2025-26 and (2) $262,100 from the General Fund for
3 FY2026-27 to the Department of Revenue, for Program 102, to aid in
4 carrying out the provisions of Legislative Bill 169, One Hundred Ninth
5 Legislature, First Session, 2025.
6 Total expenditures for permanent and temporary salaries and per
7 diems from funds appropriated in this section shall not exceed $190,900
8 for FY2025-26 or $197,000 for FY2026-27.
9 Sec. 2. Since an emergency exists, this act takes effect when
10 passed and approved according to law.
-2-
