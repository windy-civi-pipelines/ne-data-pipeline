LB262 LB262
2025 2025
LEGISLATURE OF NEBRASKA
ONE HUNDRED NINTH LEGISLATURE
FIRST SESSION
Introduced by Speaker Arch, 14; at the request of the Governor.
Read first time January 15, 2025
Committee: Appropriations
1 A BILL FOR AN ACT relating to appropriations; to appropriate funds for
2 the payment of salaries of members of the Nebraska Legislature and
3 payments to be made as provided by Chapter 68, article 6, for
4 FY2025-26 and FY2026-27; to provide an operative date; and to
5 declare an emergency.
6 Be it enacted by the people of the State of Nebraska,
-1-
LB262 LB262
2025 2025
1 Section 1. There is hereby appropriated (1) $632,982 from the
2 General Fund for FY2025-26 and (2) $632,982 from the General Fund for
3 FY2026-27 to the Legislative Council, for Program 1, for the payment of
4 salaries of members of the Nebraska Legislature and the payments to be
5 made as provided by Chapter 68, article 6.
6 Total expenditures for permanent and temporary salaries and per
7 diems from funds appropriated in this section shall not exceed $588,000
8 for FY2025-26 or $588,000 for FY2026-27.
9 Sec. 2. This act becomes operative on July 1, 2025.
10 Sec. 3. Since an emergency exists, this act takes effect when
11 passed and approved according to law.
-2-
