Bosn, 25; Bostar, 29; Brandt, 32; Cavanaugh, J., 9; Clements, 2;
Clouse, 37; DeBoer, 10; Dorn, 30; Dover, 19; Fredrickson, 20;
Guereca, 7; Hallstrom, 1; Hansen, 16; Hardin, 48; Holdcroft, 36;

31; Lippincott, 34; Lonowski, 33; McKeon, 41; McKinney, 11;
Meyer, 17; Moser, 22; Murman, 38; Prokop, 27; Quick, 35;
Raybould, 28; Riepe, 12; Rountree, 3; Sanders, 45; Sorrentino,
39; Spivey, 13; Storer, 43; Storm, 23; Strommen, 47; von Gillern,
4; Wordekemper, 15.
WHEREAS, Jordy Bahl, from the University of Nebraska-Lincoln, earned the
Big Ten Player of the Year award and the Pitcher of the Year award, the first
player to win both in a season; and
WHEREAS, Jordy's ERA is a league best of 1.46 across 168 1/3 innings
pitched to go with a record of 23-5, 243 strikeouts, and 7 shutouts; and
WHEREAS, Jordy's statistics as a hitter earned her top-ten finishes in hit
and runs, having hit .467 this season, and is on track to beat Nebraska's
batting average record; and
WHEREAS, the Legislature recognizes the academic, athletic, and artistic
achievements of the citizens of our state; and
WHEREAS, Jordy's excellence in pitching and hitting and her dedication to
softball earned her the well-deserved Big Ten Player of the Year award and the
Pitcher of the Year award.
NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE MEMBERS OF THE ONE HUNDRED NINTH
LEGISLATURE OF NEBRASKA, FIRST SESSION:

Section 2:
1. That the Legislature congratulates Jordy Bahl on earning the Big Ten
Player of the Year award and the Pitcher of the Year award.

Section 3:
2. That a copy of this resolution be sent to Jordy Bahl.
