LB502 LB502
2025 2025
LEGISLATURE OF NEBRASKA
ONE HUNDRED NINTH LEGISLATURE
FIRST SESSION
Introduced by Meyer, 17.
Read first time January 21, 2025
Committee: Agriculture
1 A BILL FOR AN ACT relating to the Farm Labor Contractors Act; to amend
2 section 48-1714, Reissue Revised Statutes of Nebraska; to provide a
3 penalty for certain seed corn producers and farm labor contractors;
4 to harmonize provisions; and to repeal the original section.
5 Be it enacted by the people of the State of Nebraska,
-1-
LB502 LB502
2025 2025
1 Section 1. Section 48-1714, Reissue Revised Statutes of Nebraska, is
2 amended to read:
3 48-1714 (1) Any person who violates violating section 48-1711 or
4 48-1712 shall be guilty of a Class II misdemeanor.
5 (2) Any person who (a) intentionally defaces, alters, or changes a
6 farm labor contractor license, (b) uses the license of another, (c)
7 knowingly permits the use of another person's license, or (d) acts as a
8 farm labor contractor without a license shall be guilty of a Class II
9 misdemeanor.
10 (3) Any seed corn producer or farm labor contractor who violates
11 section 48-1715 shall be guilty of a Class IV misdemeanor.
12 Sec. 2. Original section 48-1714, Reissue Revised Statutes of
13 Nebraska, is repealed.
-2-
