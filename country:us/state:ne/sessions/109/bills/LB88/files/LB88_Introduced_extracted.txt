LB88 LB88
2025 2025
LEGISLATURE OF NEBRASKA
ONE HUNDRED NINTH LEGISLATURE
FIRST SESSION
Introduced by Dorn, 30.
Read first time January 10, 2025
Committee: Appropriations
1 A BILL FOR AN ACT relating to appropriations; to appropriate funds to the
2 Department of Economic Development; and to state intent regarding
3 future appropriations.
4 Be it enacted by the people of the State of Nebraska,
-1-
LB88 LB88
2025 2025
1 Section 1. There is hereby appropriated (1) $500,000 from the
2 General Fund for FY2025-26 and (2) $500,000 from the General Fund for
3 FY2026-27 to the Department of Economic Development for state aid.
4 There is included in the appropriation in this section $500,000 from
5 the General Fund for FY2025-26 and $500,000 from the General Fund for
6 FY2026-27 as state aid, which shall only be used for such purpose.
7 It is the intent of the Legislature to appropriate $500,000 from the
8 General Fund annually to the Department of Economic Development to
9 contract to provide for educational programming and technical expertise
10 related to downtown or mainstreet revitalization, business growth, and
11 historic preservation to communities throughout the state. The
12 appropriation shall only be used for such purpose.
