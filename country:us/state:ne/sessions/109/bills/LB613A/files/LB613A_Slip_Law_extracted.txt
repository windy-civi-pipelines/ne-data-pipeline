Section 1:
LB613A LB613A
2025 2025
Approved by the Governor June 4, 2025
Introduced by Andersen, 49.
A BILL FOR AN ACT relating to appropriations; to appropriate funds to aid in
carrying out the provisions of Legislative Bill 613, One Hundred Ninth
Legislature, First Session, 2025.
Be it enacted by the people of the State of Nebraska,

Section 2:
Section 1. There is hereby appropriated (1) $90,200 from the General Fund
for FY2025-26 and (2) $88,000 from the General Fund for FY2026-27 to the
Department of Revenue, for Program 102, to aid in carrying out the provisions
of Legislative Bill 613, One Hundred Ninth Legislature, First Session, 2025.
Total expenditures for permanent and temporary salaries and per diems from
funds appropriated in this section shall not exceed $64,100 for FY2025-26 or
$66,200 for FY2026-27.
