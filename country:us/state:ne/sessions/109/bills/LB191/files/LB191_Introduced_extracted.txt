LB191 LB191
2025 2025
LEGISLATURE OF NEBRASKA
ONE HUNDRED NINTH LEGISLATURE
FIRST SESSION
Introduced by Bosn, 25.
Read first time January 13, 2025
Committee: Transportation and Telecommunications
1 A BILL FOR AN ACT relating to the One-Call Notification System Act; to
2 amend section 76-2325.01, Reissue Revised Statutes of Nebraska; to
3 change provisions relating to unlawful interference with
4 telecommunications; and to repeal the original section.
5 Be it enacted by the people of the State of Nebraska,
-1-
LB191 LB191
2025 2025
1 Section 1. Section 76-2325.01, Reissue Revised Statutes of Nebraska,
2 is amended to read:
3 76-2325.01 Any person who willfully and maliciously breaks, injures,
4 destroys, or otherwise interferes with the poles, wires, or other
5 facilities of any telecommunications, broadband, communications, or
6 railroad company or electric light and power company in this state or who
7 willfully and purposely interrupts or interferes with the transmission of
8 telecommunications, broadband, communications, messages or the
9 transmission of light, heat, and power in this state shall be subject to
10 the action and penalty prescribed in section 28-519.
11 Sec. 2. Original section 76-2325.01, Reissue Revised Statutes of
12 Nebraska, is repealed.
-2-
