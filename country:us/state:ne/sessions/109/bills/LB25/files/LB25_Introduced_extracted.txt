LB25 LB25
2025 2025
LEGISLATURE OF NEBRASKA
ONE HUNDRED NINTH LEGISLATURE
FIRST SESSION
Introduced by Ballard, 21.
Read first time January 09, 2025
Committee: Appropriations
1 A BILL FOR AN ACT relating to appropriations; to appropriate funds to the
2 Department of Administrative Services; and to declare an emergency.
3 Be it enacted by the people of the State of Nebraska,
-1-
LB25 LB25
2025 2025
1 Section 1. There is hereby appropriated $5,000,000 from the General
2 Fund for FY2025-26 to the Department of Administrative Services, for
3 Program 245, for state aid as described in this section, which shall only
4 be used for such purpose.
5 There is included in the appropriation to this program for FY2025-26
6 $5,000,000 General Funds, which shall be used for providing for
7 interoperable communication between volunteer departments as defined in
8 section 35-901 through a grant program. Grants shall be used to provide
9 mobile radios, programming, and installation of radios and equipment and
10 shall be made on a one-to-one match for local funds committed to
11 providing for interoperability. An applicant receiving a grant under this
12 section shall not be awarded more than $3,500,000.
13 Sec. 2. Since an emergency exists, this act takes effect when
14 passed and approved according to law.
-2-
