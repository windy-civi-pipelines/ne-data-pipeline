LB467 LB467
2025 2025
LEGISLATURE OF NEBRASKA
ONE HUNDRED NINTH LEGISLATURE
FIRST SESSION
Introduced by Riepe, 12.
Read first time January 21, 2025
Committee: Banking, Commerce and Insurance
1 A BILL FOR AN ACT relating to the Utilization Review Act; to amend
2 section 44-5416, Reissue Revised Statutes of Nebraska; to provide a
3 duty to implement a prior authorization application programming
4 interface; to harmonize provisions; and to repeal the original
5 section.
6 Be it enacted by the people of the State of Nebraska,
-1-
LB467 LB467
2025 2025
1 Section 1. (1) A health carrier or utilization review agent shall,
2 on or before January 1, 2028, implement and maintain a prior
3 authorization application programming interface, pursuant to 45 C.F.R.
4 156.223(b), as such regulation existed on January 1, 2025, to be in
5 effect on January 1, 2028.
6 (2) Nothing in this section shall be construed to apply to a prior
7 authorization request for coverage of drugs.
8 (3) As used in this section, drug means the same as defined in 45
9 C.F.R. 156.221(b)(1)(v), as such regulation existed on January 1, 2025.
10 Sec. 2. Section 44-5416, Reissue Revised Statutes of Nebraska, is
11 amended to read:
12 44-5416 Sections 44-5416 to 44-5431 and section 1 of this act shall
13 be known and may be cited as the Utilization Review Act.
14 Sec. 3. Original section 44-5416, Reissue Revised Statutes of
15 Nebraska, is repealed.
-2-
