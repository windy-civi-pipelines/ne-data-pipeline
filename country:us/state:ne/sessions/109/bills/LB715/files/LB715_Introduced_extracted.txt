LEGISLATURE OF NEBRASKA
ONE HUNDRED NINTH LEGISLATURE
FIRST SESSION
Introduced by Hunt, 8; Bostar, 29.
Read first time January 22, 2025
Committee: Banking, Commerce and Insurance
1 A BILL FOR AN ACT relating to insurance; to require coverage for pre-
2 exposure prophylaxis medication as prescribed.
3 Be it enacted by the people of the State of Nebraska,
-1-
LB715 LB715
2025 2025
1 Section 1. (1) Notwithstanding section 44-3,131, (a) any individual
2 or group sickness and accident insurance policy or subscriber contract
3 delivered, issued for delivery, or renewed in this state and any
4 hospital, medical, or surgical expense-incurred policy, except for
5 policies that provide coverage for a specified disease or other limited-
6 benefit coverage, and (b) any self-funded employee benefit plan to the
7 extent not preempted by federal law shall include coverage for pre-
8 exposure prophylaxis medication for the prevention of human
9 immunodeficiency virus infection if prescribed by a physician or health
10 care professional legally authorized by law to prescribe such medication.
11 (2) This section does not prevent application of deductible or
12 copayment provisions or network incentives contained in the policy,
13 contract, or plan.
