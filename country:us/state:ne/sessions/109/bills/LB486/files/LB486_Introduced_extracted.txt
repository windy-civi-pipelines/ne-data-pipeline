LEGISLATURE OF NEBRASKA
ONE HUNDRED NINTH LEGISLATURE
FIRST SESSION
Introduced by Quick, 35.
Read first time January 21, 2025
Committee: Health and Human Services
1 A BILL FOR AN ACT relating to health and human services; to provide
2 duties for the Department of Health and Human Services regarding
3 reimbursement for certain service providers.
4 Be it enacted by the people of the State of Nebraska,
-1-
LB486 LB486
2025 2025
1 Section 1. The Department of Health and Human Services shall
2 implement a standard reimbursement rate for service providers under the
3 aged and disabled personal care services waiver. Such rate shall be
4 consistent with the rate study report provided to the State of Nebraska
5 by CBIZ Optumas dated December 7, 2022, and shall be rebased not less
6 than once every two years.
