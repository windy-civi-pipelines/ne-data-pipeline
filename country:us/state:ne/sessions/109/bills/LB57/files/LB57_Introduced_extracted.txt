LEGISLATURE OF NEBRASKA
ONE HUNDRED NINTH LEGISLATURE
FIRST SESSION
Introduced by Dorn, 30.
Read first time January 09, 2025
Committee: Appropriations
1 A BILL FOR AN ACT relating to appropriations; to state intent regarding
2 appropriations to the Department of Health and Human Services for
3 medicaid assisted-living facilities.
4 Be it enacted by the people of the State of Nebraska,
-1-
LB57 LB57
2025 2025
1 Section 1. It is the intent of the Legislature to appropriate (1)
2 $7,926,576 for FY2025-26 ($3,345,808 General Funds and $4,580,768 Federal
3 Funds) and (2) $8,243,639 for FY2026-27 ($3,479,640 General Funds and
4 $4,763,999 Federal Funds) to the Department of Health and Human Services
5 for medicaid assisted-living facility rates for Program 348, to be used
6 specifically by the department to increase the current medicaid assisted-
7 living facility rates, which are included in Program No. 348 and paid as
8 a service as part of Nebraska's aged and disabled home and community-
9 based medicaid 1915(c) waiver program, to the daily rate of $88.24 for
10 FY2025-26 and $91.78 for FY2026-27, for both rural and urban assisted-
11 living waiver days.
