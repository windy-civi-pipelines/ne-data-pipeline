LEGISLATURE OF NEBRASKA
ONE HUNDRED NINTH LEGISLATURE
FIRST SESSION
Introduced by Cavanaugh, J., 9.
Read first time January 22, 2025
Committee: Nebraska Retirement Systems
1 A BILL FOR AN ACT relating to public power districts; to require cost-of-
2 living adjustments for any public power district that operates a
3 defined benefit plan.
4 Be it enacted by the people of the State of Nebraska,
-1-
LB571 LB571
2025 2025
1 Section 1. Beginning January 1, 2026, a public power district
2 operating a defined benefit plan shall pay a cost-of-living adjustment
3 equal to the annual cost-of-living adjustment determined by the federal
4 Social Security Administration to any retiree who is not eligible to
5 receive social security benefits.
