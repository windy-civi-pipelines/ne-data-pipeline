LEGISLATURE OF NEBRASKA
ONE HUNDRED NINTH LEGISLATURE
FIRST SESSION
Introduced by Murman, 38.
Read first time May 19, 2025
1 A BILL FOR AN ACT relating to appropriations; to appropriate funds to aid
2 in carrying out the provisions of Legislative Bill 306, One Hundred
3 Ninth Legislature, First Session, 2025.
4 Be it enacted by the people of the State of Nebraska,
-1-
LB306A LB306A
2025 2025
1 Section 1. There is hereby appropriated (1) $2,000 from the Auditor
2 of Public Accounts Cash Fund for FY2025-26 and (2) $5,000 from the
3 Auditor of Public Accounts Cash Fund for FY2026-27 to the Auditor of
4 Public Accounts, for Program 525, to aid in carrying out the provisions
5 of Legislative Bill 306, One Hundred Ninth Legislature, First Session,
6 2025.
7 Total expenditures for permanent and temporary salaries and per
8 diems from funds appropriated in this section shall not exceed $1,900 for
9 FY2025-26 or $4,700 for FY2026-27.
10 Sec. 2. There is hereby appropriated (1) $569,833 from the General
11 Fund for FY2025-26 and (2) $410,981 from the General Fund for FY2026-27
12 to the State Department of Education, for Program 25, to aid in carrying
13 out the provisions of Legislative Bill 306, One Hundred Ninth
14 Legislature, First Session, 2025.
15 Total expenditures for permanent and temporary salaries and per
16 diems from funds appropriated in this section shall not exceed $304,566
17 for FY2025-26 or $226,122 for FY2026-27.
18 Sec. 3. There is hereby appropriated (1) $250,000 from the General
19 Fund for FY2025-26 and (2) $250,000 from the General Fund for FY2026-27
20 to the State Department of Education, for Program 158, to aid in carrying
21 out the provisions of Legislative Bill 306, One Hundred Ninth
22 Legislature, First Session, 2025.
23 There is included in the appropriation to this program for FY2025-26
24 $250,000 General Funds for state aid, which shall only be used for such
25 purpose. There is included in the appropriation to this program for
26 FY2026-27 $250,000 General Funds for state aid, which shall only be used
27 for such purpose
28 No expenditures for permanent and temporary salaries and per diems
29 for state employees shall be made from funds appropriated in this
30 section.
31 Sec. 4. There is hereby appropriated (1) $192,800 from the General
