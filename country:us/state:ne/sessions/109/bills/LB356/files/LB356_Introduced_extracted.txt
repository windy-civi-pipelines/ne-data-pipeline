LEGISLATURE OF NEBRASKA
ONE HUNDRED NINTH LEGISLATURE
FIRST SESSION
Introduced by General Affairs Committee: Holdcroft, 36, Chairperson;
Andersen, 49; Clouse, 37; DeKay, 40; Quick, 35; Rountree,
3; Storm, 23.
Read first time January 16, 2025
Committee: Executive Board
1 A BILL FOR AN ACT relating to the Nebraska Arts Council; to amend section
2 82-309, Reissue Revised Statutes of Nebraska; to change provisions
3 relating to the appointment of members to the council; and to repeal
4 the original section.
5 Be it enacted by the people of the State of Nebraska,
-1-
LB356 LB356
2025 2025
1 Section 1. Section 82-309, Reissue Revised Statutes of Nebraska, is
2 amended to read:
3 82-309 There is hereby created the Nebraska Arts Council to consist
4 of fifteen members to be appointed by the Governor with the approval of
5 the Legislature from among citizens of Nebraska who are known for their
6 professional competence and experience in connection with the arts. In
7 making such appointments, consideration shall be given to recommendations
8 made by representative civic, educational, and professional associations
9 and groups concerned with, or engaged in, the production or presentation
10 of the arts generally.
11 Sec. 2. Original section 82-309, Reissue Revised Statutes of
12 Nebraska, is repealed.
