LB527A LB527A
2025 2025
LEGISLATURE OF NEBRASKA
ONE HUNDRED NINTH LEGISLATURE
FIRST SESSION
Introduced by Jacobson, 42.
Read first time March 11, 2025
1 A BILL FOR AN ACT relating to appropriations; to appropriate funds to aid
2 in carrying out the provisions of Legislative Bill 527, One Hundred
3 Ninth Legislature, First Session, 2025; and to declare an emergency.
4 Be it enacted by the people of the State of Nebraska,
-1-
LB527A LB527A
2025 2025
1 Section 1. There is hereby appropriated (1) $63,157,578 from the
2 Medicaid Access and Quality Fund and $117,292,645 from federal funds for
3 FY2025-26 and (2) $128,815,156 from the Medicaid Access and Quality Fund
4 and $239,228,148 from federal funds for FY2026-27 to the Department of
5 Health and Human Services, for Program 348, to aid in carrying out the
6 provisions of Legislative Bill 527, One Hundred Ninth Legislature, First
7 Session, 2025.
8 There is included in the appropriation to this program for FY2025-26
9 $63,157,578 Cash Funds and $117,292,645 federal funds for state aid,
10 which shall only be used for such purpose. There is included in the
11 appropriation to this program for FY2026-27 $128,815,156 Cash Funds and
12 $239,228,148 federal funds for state aid, which shall only be used for
13 such purpose.
14 No expenditures for permanent and temporary salaries and per diems
15 for state employees shall be made from funds appropriated in this
16 section.
17 Sec. 2. Since an emergency exists, this act takes effect when
18 passed and approved according to law.
