LEGISLATURE OF NEBRASKA
ONE HUNDRED NINTH LEGISLATURE
FIRST SESSION
FINAL READING
Introduced by Jacobson, 42.
Read first time March 11, 2025
1 A BILL FOR AN ACT relating to appropriations; to appropriate funds to aid
2 in carrying out the provisions of Legislative Bill 527, One Hundred
3 Ninth Legislature, First Session, 2025; and to declare an emergency.
4 Be it enacted by the people of the State of Nebraska,
-1-
LB527A LB527A
2025 2025
1 Section 1. There is hereby appropriated (1) $5,765,385 from the
2 Medicaid Access and Quality Fund and $12,279,638 from federal funds for
3 FY2025-26 and (2) $11,758,984 from the Medicaid Access and Quality Fund
4 and $25,045,347 from federal funds for FY2026-27 to the Department of
5 Health and Human Services, for Program 344, to aid in carrying out the
6 provisions of Legislative Bill 527, One Hundred Ninth Legislature, First
7 Session, 2025.
8 There is included in the appropriation to this program for FY2025-26
9 $5,765,385 Cash Funds and $12,279,638 federal funds for state aid, which
10 shall only be used for such purpose. There is included in the
11 appropriation to this program for FY2026-27 $11,758,984 Cash Funds and
12 $25,045,347 federal funds for state aid, which shall only be used for
13 such purpose.
14 No expenditures for permanent and temporary salaries and per diems
15 for state employees shall be made from funds appropriated in this
16 section.
17 Sec. 2. There is hereby appropriated (1) $57,392,193 from the
18 Medicaid Access and Quality Fund and $105,013,007 from federal funds for
19 FY2025-26 and (2) $117,056,172 from the Medicaid Access and Quality Fund
20 and $214,182,801 from federal funds for FY2026-27 to the Department of
21 Health and Human Services, for Program 348, to aid in carrying out the
22 provisions of Legislative Bill 527, One Hundred Ninth Legislature, First
23 Session, 2025.
24 There is included in the appropriation to this program for FY2025-26
25 $57,392,193 Cash Funds and $105,013,007 federal funds for state aid,
26 which shall only be used for such purpose. There is included in the
27 appropriation to this program for FY2026-27 $117,056,172 Cash Funds and
28 $214,182,801 federal funds for state aid, which shall only be used for
29 such purpose.
30 No expenditures for permanent and temporary salaries and per diems
31 for state employees shall be made from funds appropriated in this
-2-

2 Sec. 3. Since an emergency exists, this act takes effect when
3 passed and approved according to law.
