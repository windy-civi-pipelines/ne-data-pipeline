LB22A LB22A
2025 2025
LEGISLATURE OF NEBRASKA
ONE HUNDRED NINTH LEGISLATURE
FIRST SESSION
Introduced by Dungan, 26.
Read first time March 12, 2025
1 A BILL FOR AN ACT relating to appropriations; to appropriate funds to aid
2 in carrying out the provisions of Legislative Bill 22, One Hundred
3 Ninth Legislature, First Session, 2025.
4 Be it enacted by the people of the State of Nebraska,
-1-
LB22A LB22A
2025 2025
1 Section 1. There is hereby appropriated (1) $84,428 from the
2 Medicaid Managed Care Excess Profit Fund and $184,472 from federal funds
3 for FY2025-26 and (2) $171,827 from the Medicaid Managed Care Excess
4 Profit Fund and $365,973 from federal funds for FY2026-27 to the
5 Department of Health and Human Services, for Program 344, to aid in
6 carrying out the provisions of Legislative Bill 22, One Hundred Ninth
7 Legislature, First Session, 2025.
8 There is included in the appropriation to this program for FY2025-26
9 $84,428 Cash Funds and $184,472 federal funds for state aid, which shall
10 only be used for such purpose. There is included in the appropriation to
11 this program for FY2026-27 $171,827 Cash Funds and $365,973 federal funds
12 for state aid, which shall only be used for such purpose.
13 No expenditures for permanent and temporary salaries and per diems
14 for state employees shall be made from funds appropriated in this
15 section.
16 Sec. 2. There is hereby appropriated (1) $262,715 from the Medicaid
17 Managed Care Excess Profit Fund and $543,985 from federal funds for
18 FY2025-26 and (2) $533,928 from the Medicaid Managed Care Excess Profit
19 Fund and $1,079,472 from federal funds for FY2026-27 to the Department of
20 Health and Human Services, for Program 348, to aid in carrying out the
21 provisions of Legislative Bill 22, One Hundred Ninth Legislature, First
22 Session, 2025.
23 There is included in the appropriation to this program for FY2025-26
24 $262,715 Cash Funds and $543,985 federal funds for state aid, which shall
25 only be used for such purpose. There is included in the appropriation to
26 this program for FY2026-27 $533,928 Cash Funds and $1,079,472 federal
27 funds for state aid, which shall only be used for such purpose.
28 No expenditures for permanent and temporary salaries and per diems
29 for state employees shall be made from funds appropriated in this
30 section.
