ONE HUNDRED NINTH LEGISLATURE
FIRST SESSION
LEGISLATIVE RESOLUTION 6CA
PROPOSED CONSTITUTIONAL AMENDMENT
Read first time January 10, 2025
Committee: Judiciary
1 THE MEMBERS OF THE ONE HUNDRED NINTH LEGISLATURE OF NEBRASKA, FIRST
2 SESSION, RESOLVE THAT:
3 Section 1. At the general election in November 2026, the following
4 proposed amendment to the Constitution of Nebraska shall be submitted to
5 the electors of the State of Nebraska for approval or rejection:
6 To amend Article I, section 29:
7 I-29 The State of Nebraska and its political subdivisions shall
8 recognize marriage and issue marriage licenses to couples regardless of
9 gender and race. Only marriage between a man and a woman shall be valid
10 or recognized in Nebraska. The uniting of two persons of the same sex in
11 a civil union, domestic partnership, or other similar same-sex
12 relationship shall not be valid or recognized in Nebraska.
13 Sec. 2. The proposed amendment shall be submitted to the electors
14 in the manner prescribed by the Constitution of Nebraska, Article XVI,
15 section 1, with the following ballot language:
16 A constitutional amendment to change provisions relating to
17 marriage.
18 For
19 Against.
