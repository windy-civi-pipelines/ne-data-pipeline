LB86 LB86
2025 2025
LEGISLATURE OF NEBRASKA
ONE HUNDRED NINTH LEGISLATURE
FIRST SESSION
Introduced by Dorn, 30.
Read first time January 10, 2025
Committee: Appropriations
1 A BILL FOR AN ACT relating to appropriations; to appropriate funds to the
2 Department of Natural Resources; and to declare an emergency.
3 Be it enacted by the people of the State of Nebraska,
-1-
LB86 LB86
2025 2025
1 Section 1. There is hereby appropriated $500,000 from the General
2 Fund for FY2025-26 to the Department of Natural Resources, for Program
3 334, for the purpose of issuing a grant to a natural resources district
4 that has more than two hundred fifty watershed structures in order to
5 fund projects involving the operation, maintenance, or repair of such
6 structures. A project is eligible for grant funding under this section if
7 the total cost of the project exceeds thirty thousand dollars per
8 structure.
9 There is included in the appropriation to this program for FY2025-26
10 $500,000 General Funds for state aid, which shall only be used for such
11 purpose.
12 Sec. 2. Since an emergency exists, this act takes effect when
13 passed and approved according to law.
-2-
