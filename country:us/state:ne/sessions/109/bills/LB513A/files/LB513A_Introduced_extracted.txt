LEGISLATURE OF NEBRASKA
ONE HUNDRED NINTH LEGISLATURE
FIRST SESSION
Introduced by Bosn, 25.
Read first time May 12, 2025
1 A BILL FOR AN ACT relating to appropriations; to appropriate funds to aid
2 in carrying out the provisions of Legislative Bill 513, One Hundred
3 Ninth Legislature, First Session, 2025; and to declare an emergency.
4 Be it enacted by the people of the State of Nebraska,
-1-
LB513A LB513A
2025 2025
1 Section 1. There is hereby appropriated (1) $26,838 from the
2 General Fund for FY2025-26 and (2) $53,775 from the General Fund for
3 FY2026-27 to the Supreme Court, for Program 3, to aid in carrying out the
4 provisions of Legislative Bill 513, One Hundred Ninth Legislature, First
5 Session, 2025.
6 Total expenditures for permanent and temporary salaries and per
7 diems from funds appropriated in this section shall not exceed $23,631
8 for FY2025-26 or $47,616 for FY2026-27.
9 Sec. 2. There is hereby appropriated (1) $21,976 from the General
10 Fund for FY2025-26 and (2) $44,023 from the General Fund for FY2026-27 to
11 the Supreme Court, for Program 4, to aid in carrying out the provisions
12 of Legislative Bill 513, One Hundred Ninth Legislature, First Session,
13 2025.
14 Total expenditures for permanent and temporary salaries and per
15 diems from funds appropriated in this section shall not exceed $19,242
16 for FY2025-26 or $38,774 for FY2026-27.
17 Sec. 3. There is hereby appropriated (1) $250,398 from the General
18 Fund for FY2025-26 and (2) $501,519 from the General Fund for FY2026-27
19 to the Supreme Court, for Program 6, to aid in carrying out the
20 provisions of Legislative Bill 513, One Hundred Ninth Legislature, First
21 Session, 2025.
22 Total expenditures for permanent and temporary salaries and per
23 diems from funds appropriated in this section shall not exceed $218,585
24 for FY2025-26 or $440,449 for FY2026-27.
25 Sec. 4. There is hereby appropriated (1) $205,999 from the General
26 Fund for FY2025-26 and (2) $412,530 from the General Fund for FY2026-27
27 to the Supreme Court, for Program 7, to aid in carrying out the
28 provisions of Legislative Bill 513, One Hundred Ninth Legislature, First
29 Session, 2025.
30 Total expenditures for permanent and temporary salaries and per
31 diems from funds appropriated in this section shall not exceed $179,257
-2-

1 for FY2025-26 or $361,202 for FY2026-27.
2 Sec. 5. There is hereby appropriated (1) $21,574 from the
3 Compensation Court Cash Fund for FY2025-26 and (2) $43,211 from the
4 Compensation Court Cash Fund for FY2026-27 to the Nebraska Workers'
5 Compensation Court, for Program 526, to aid in carrying out the
6 provisions of Legislative Bill 513, One Hundred Ninth Legislature, First
7 Session, 2025.
8 Total expenditures for permanent and temporary salaries and per
9 diems from funds appropriated in this section shall not exceed $18,736
10 for FY2025-26 or $37,753 for FY2026-27.
11 Sec. 6. There is hereby appropriated (1) $12,051 from the General
12 Fund for FY2025-26 and (2) $24,283 from the General Fund for FY2026-27 to
13 the Tax Equalization and Review Commission, for Program 131, to aid in
14 carrying out the provisions of Legislative Bill 513, One Hundred Ninth
15 Legislature, First Session, 2025.
16 Total expenditures for permanent and temporary salaries and per
17 diems from funds appropriated in this section shall not exceed $10,465
18 for FY2025-26 or $21,087 for FY2026-27.
19 Sec. 7. Since an emergency exists, this act takes effect when
20 passed and approved according to law.
